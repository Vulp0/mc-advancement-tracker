Minecraft Advancement Tracker (WIP)

How to use:

1.- Open the file explorer and go to your .minecraft folder, this will depend on your OS

Windows Linux
C:\Users\yourUser\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft /home/user/.minecraft

2.- Go to your saves folder, open your world folder, then into advancements

3.- Copy the path to your advancements folder, then click below to upload a file

4.- Paste the path into your file explorer, then upload the json file (it will have a weird name like d761008a-1d69-4a77-838d-2bbdc4c099f7)

5.- Press check for advancements and open the console for the results (right click anywhere, then inspect, then console)
